Restrictions:

  • No alcohol or drugs
  • No buses or RVs
  • No trailors
  • No monster trucks
  • No extreme tires
  • Normal truck beds must be empty
  • All doors, roofs, and windows must be installed
  • No pets
  • No outside animal feed
  • In the event of high winds, lightning, or tonado watches and warnings, Harmony Park Safari will be closed.
